About

We are a Malaysian restaurant focused on local produce and culture in a modern and contemporary way. Li opened in March 2015 in the heart of Damansara Jaya. Inspired by our Malaysian roots, Li looks to bring Malaysian produce and culture into the future in a casual and innovative way while respecting the past. Over the years our space has evolved into a modern Malaysian restaurant/wine bar featuring sharing meat dishes, vegetables from the market, and eclectic drinks sourced from our team.
